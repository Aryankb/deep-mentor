Okay, chalo dekhte hain **Potential Energy of a Dipole Placed in a Uniform Electric Field** exam ke liye kaise prepare karna hai.

**Key Concepts (Mukhya baatein):**

*   **Electric Dipole:** Do equal aur opposite charges (+q aur -q) ek chote distance se separated. Example: HCL molecule.
*   **Uniform Electric Field:** Electric field jiska magnitude aur direction same ho har point pe.

**Real World Applications (Asli Zindagi mein istemal):**

*   **Microwave Ovens:** Water molecules (jo ki dipoles hain) rotate hote hain electric field mein, jisse heat produce hoti hai.
*   **Material properties**: Many materials have a sepcific dipole moment which defines it's properties

**Formula (sootr):**

*   **Torque (ghumaav) on a dipole:** \(\vec{\tau} = \vec{p} \times \vec{E}\)  (\(\tau = pE \sin\theta\)). Yahan,  `p` dipole moment hai, `E` electric field hai, aur `θ` angle hai dipole aur field ke beech mein.
*   **Potential Energy (shakti):** \(U = -\vec{p} \cdot \vec{E} = -pE \cos\theta\). Iska matlab, jab dipole electric field ke parallel hai (θ = 0°), potential energy minimum hoti hai (-pE), aur jab perpendicular hai (θ = 90°), potential energy zero hoti hai.

**Common Question Patterns (aam taur par pooche jaane waale sawaal):**

1.  **Torque Calculation:** Tumhe dipole moment aur electric field diya jaega, aur torque calculate karne ko kaha jaega.
    *   *Approach:* Formula use karo \(\tau = pE \sin\theta\). Angle ka dhyan rakhna.
2.  **Potential Energy Calculation:** Dipole moment, electric field aur angle diya jaega, potential energy nikalni hogi.
    *   *Approach:* Formula use karo \(U = -pE \cos\theta\). Angle ka dhyan rakhna.
3.  **Stability:** Dipole kab stable equilibrium mein hai aur kab unstable mein?
    *   *Approach:* Stable equilibrium jab  θ = 0° (U = -pE, minimum). Unstable equilibrium jab θ = 180° (U = pE, maximum).
4.  **Work Done:** Dipole ko ek angle se dusre angle tak rotate karne mein kitna work karna padega?
    *   *Approach:* Work done = Change in potential energy = \(U_2 - U_1 = -pE(\cos\theta_2 - \cos\theta_1)\)

**How to Approach Problems (samasyaon ko kaise hal karein):**

1.  **Identify Given Values:** Sawaal mein kya diya hai (p, E, θ)?
2.  **Choose Correct Formula:** Torque nikalna hai ya potential energy?
3.  **Substitute Values:** Formula mein values sahi se daalo.
4.  **Calculate:** Answer nikaalo aur units ka dhyan rakho.

**Quick Revision Points (last minute revision ke liye):**

*   **Torque:**  tendency to align dipole with the electric field.
*   **Potential Energy:** Minimum when dipole is aligned with field, maximum when anti-aligned.
*   **Stable Equilibrium:** θ = 0°.
*   **Unstable Equilibrium:** θ = 180°.
*   **Work Done:**  rotate karne mein, change in potential energy.

**Theoretical Topics (jyada important nahi hai):**

*   **Derivation of Formulas:** Formula kaise derive hua, yaad rakhne ki zarurat nahi. Seedha formula use karo.
*   **Detailed Explanation of Dipole Moment:** Dipole moment kya hota hai, iski definition pata honi chahiye, zyada detail mein jaane ki zarurat nahi.
