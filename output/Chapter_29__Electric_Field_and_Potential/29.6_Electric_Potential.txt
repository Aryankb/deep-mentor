Okay, let's get you prepped for your exam on Electric Potential! Hum Electric Potential ko easy bhasha mein samjhenge, formulas yaad karenge, aur dekhenge ki exam mein kaise questions aate hain.

**Key Concepts (Mukhya baatein)**

*   **Electric Potential Kya Hai?** Imagine electric field ek hill jaise hai, toh potential height jaise hai. Electric potential ek point par, yeh batata hai ki ek unit positive charge ko infinity se uss point tak lane mein kitna work karna padega. Basically, it's electric potential energy per unit charge.

*   **Potential Difference:** Do points ke beech potential difference matlab, ek charge ko ek point se dusre point tak le jaane mein kitna work karna padega per unit charge.

**Formulas (Important ones)**

*   **Potential Difference:** `VB - VA = (UB - UA) / q`  (VB aur VA potential hain points B aur A par, UB aur UA potential energy hain, aur q charge hai).

*   **Potential due to a point charge Q at distance r:** `V = Q / (4πε₀r)`  (ε₀ permittivity of free space hai, yeh constant hai).

*   **Relation between Electric Field and Potential:** `dV = - E ⋅ dr` or `V2 - V1 = - ∫E ⋅ dr` (This tells you how potential changes as you move in an electric field).

**Real-World Applications (Kahan kaam aata hai?)**

*   **Electronics:** Circuits mein voltage potential difference hi toh hai!
*   **Medical equipment:** ECG, EEG mein bhi potential difference measure hota hai.
*   **Lightning:** Clouds aur ground ke beech potential difference jab bahut zyada ho jaata hai toh lightning hoti hai.

**Common Question Patterns (Exam mein kaise sawaal aate hain?)**

1.  **Potential Due to a Point Charge/System of Charges:**
    *   **Question:** Charges diye honge, aur kisi point par potential nikalne ko bolenge.
    *   **Approach:** Har charge ka potential nikaalo, aur phir add kar do (potential ek scalar quantity hai, toh direction ka tension nahi hai).

2.  **Potential Difference and Work Done:**
    *   **Question:** Potential difference diya hoga, charge diya hoga, aur work done nikalne ko bolenge.
    *   **Approach:** `W = q * (VB - VA)` formula use karo.

3.  **Electric Field and Potential Relation:**
    *   **Question:** Electric field diya hoga, potential difference nikalne ko bolenge (integration use karna padega). Ya phir, potential diya hoga aur electric field nikalne ko bolenge.
    *   **Approach:** `E = -dV/dr` (agar electric field ek direction mein hai).

**Example Question**

Do charges, q1 = +5µC and q2 = -3µC, are placed 40 cm apart. Determine the potential at a point midway between them.

**Solution:**
*Step 1: Find the distance from each charge to the midpoint.*
Since the point is midway, the distance from each charge is 40cm / 2 = 20cm = 0.2m

*Step 2: Calculate the potential due to each charge at the midpoint.*
V1 = (9 x 10^9 Nm²/C²) * (5 x 10^-6 C) / 0.2m = 225,000 V
V2 = (9 x 10^9 Nm²/C²) * (-3 x 10^-6 C) / 0.2m = -135,000 V

*Step 3: Add the individual potentials to find the net potential.*
V_net = V1 + V2 = 225,000 V - 135,000 V = 90,000 V

**Quick Revision Points (Last Minute Tips)**

*   Potential ek scalar hai, electric field vector hai.
*   Potential due to positive charge is positive, due to negative charge is negative.
*   Equipotential surface matlab, surface jahan potential same hai.
*   Electric field lines are always perpendicular to equipotential surfaces.
