**Key Concepts & Formulas (तुरंत याद रखने वाले):**

*   **Electric Field Lines (electric force lines):** yeh imaginary lines hain jo electric field ki direction batati hain. Kisi bhi point par, line par tangent draw karo, woh aapko electric field ki direction dega.
*   Lines indicate the direction of the force on a positive test charge placed in the field
    *Electric force lines ek region mein electric field ko graph bana ke dikhati hain, jisko electric force lines bolte hain. Force ki lines aise banti hain ki force line ki tangent resultant electric field ki direction deti hain.

*   **Direction:**
    *   Positive charge se lines *nikalti* hain (emanate).
    *   Negative charge mein lines *terminate* hoti hain.

*   **Intensity:** Lines ki density (kitni paas-paas hain) electric field ki strength batati hai. Lines jitni close hongi, field utna strong hoga. Agar lines uniformly draw ki jaati hain, toh do points par field ki intensity compare karne ke liye distribution ko dekha jaata hai.

**Real-World Applications (kahan kaam aate hain?):**

*   **Electrostatic painting:** Lines of force paint particles ko object tak guide karti hain.
*   **Lightning arresters:** Lines of force ko redirect karke building ko bachate hain.

**Common Question Patterns & How to Approach Them:**

1.  **Drawing Field Lines:**
    *   **Question:** Draw the electric field lines for a system of two equal and opposite charges.
    *   **Approach:**
        *   Positive charge se lines *nikalo*.
        *   Negative charge mein lines *daalo*.
        *   Lines kabhi bhi intersect nahi karti.
        *   Lines ki density charge ke paas zyada honi chahiye.
        *   Draw lines symmetrically.
        *   basically dipole moment ke jaisa banana hai

2.  **Intensity Comparison:**
    *   **Question:** Point A aur B diye hain. Field lines dekh kar batao kahan electric field strong hai.
    *   **Approach:**
        *   Dekho kahan lines zyada dense hain. Jahan lines paas-paas hain, wahan field strong hai.

**Quick Revision Points (exam se pehle):**

*   Electric field lines kabhi intersect nahi karti hain.
*   Lines positive se shuru hoti hain aur negative par end hoti hain.
*   Lines ki density field ki strength batati hai.
*   Uniform electric field mein lines parallel aur evenly spaced hoti hain.
*   Lines of force real nahi hoti, sirf electric field ko visualise karne ka ek tareeka hain.

**Important point:**

*The lines of force are purely a geometrical construction which help us to visualise the nature of electric field in a region. They have no physical existence.*

**Example Question:**

Do points diye hain, P1 aur P2. P1 par 10 lines pass ho rahi hain ek area se, aur P2 par 5 lines. Kahan electric field strong hai aur kitna strong hai?

**Answer:**

P1 par electric field strong hai because wahan lines ki density zyada hai. Electric field P1 par P2 se roughly double hoga, kyunki lines ki sankhya double hai.
