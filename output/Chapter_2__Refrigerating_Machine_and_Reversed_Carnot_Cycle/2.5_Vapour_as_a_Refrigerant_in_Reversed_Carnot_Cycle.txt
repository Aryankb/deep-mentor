Okay, chalo dekhte hain "Vapour as a Refrigerant in Reversed Carnot Cycle" kaise padhna hai exam ke liye. Yeh topic refrigeration cycle mein vapour (basically gas) ko kaise use karte hain, uske baare mein hai.

**Key Concepts aur Real-World Applications**

*   **Reversed Carnot Cycle:** Imagine ek engine jo ulta kaam karta hai. Normal engine heat se work karta hai, but yeh cycle work se heat transfer karta hai. Basically, yeh ek ideal refrigeration cycle hai.

    *   **Real-world application:** AC aur fridge isi principle pe kaam karte hain, although actual cycles thode different hote hain.

*   **Vapour as Refrigerant:** Hum ek special liquid (refrigerant) use karte hain jo easily vaporize (gas banta hai) aur condense (liquid banta hai). Example: R-12 (although abhi use nahi hota), R-134a, etc.

    *   **Why Vapour?** Kyunki liquid se gas banne aur gas se liquid banne mein heat absorb aur release hoti hai, jisse hum cooling effect paate hain.

*   **Four Processes:** Is cycle mein char main cheezein hoti hain:
    1.  **Evaporation (4-1):** Refrigerant liquid se gas banta hai, heat absorb karke.
    2.  **Isentropic Compression (1-2):** Gas ko compress karte hain bina heat add ya remove kiye.
    3.  **Condensation (2-3):** Refrigerant gas se liquid banta hai, heat release karke.
    4.  **Isentropic Expansion (3-4):** Liquid ko expand karte hain bina heat add ya remove kiye.

*   **Isothermal Process:** Isothermal ka matlab hota hai constant temperature process

**Important Formulas:**

*   **COP (Coefficient of Performance):** Yeh batata hai ki cycle kitna efficient hai.
    *   `COP = Desired Cooling Effect / Work Input = qo / |w|`

    *   `qo = h1 - h4` (Refrigerating effect)
    *   `|w| = (h2 – h1) – (h3 - h4)` (Net work)

**Common Question Patterns aur Approach**

1.  **Cycle Diagram:** Aapko T-s (Temperature-entropy) diagram banana ya pehchanna aa sakta hai.
    *   Diagram mein, evaporation horizontal line hoti hai (constant temperature), compression upar ki taraf, condensation horizontal line, aur expansion neeche ki taraf.
2.  **COP Calculation:** Aapko temperatures diye jayenge aur COP calculate karna hoga.
    *   Carnot cycle ke liye: `COP = Tc / (Th - Tc)`  (Tc = cold reservoir temperature, Th = hot reservoir temperature)
3.  **Process Identification:** Aapko ek process describe kiya jayega aur aapko pehchanna hoga ki yeh kaunsa process hai (evaporation, condensation, etc.).

**Quick Revision Points**

*   Reversed Carnot cycle ek ideal cycle hai.
*   Refrigerant ka vaporization aur condensation cooling effect produce karta hai.
*   COP jitna zyada, cycle utna efficient.
*   T-s diagram mein processes ko pehchanana important hai.

**Example Question Approach**

Agar question mein diya hai ki ek Carnot refrigerator -30°C aur 35°C ke beech kaam kar raha hai, aur aapko COP nikalna hai:

1.  **Temperatures ko Kelvin mein convert karo:**
    *   Tc = -30 + 273 = 243 K
    *   Th = 35 + 273 = 308 K
2.  **COP ka formula use karo:**
    *   COP = 243 / (308 - 243) = 243 / 65 = 3.74

**Theoretical Topics(Over view)**

*   The reversed Carnot cycle can be made almost completely practical by operating in the liquid-vapour region of a pure substance.
*   Indiscriminate use of air-cooled window-type air conditioners is wasteful of energy and equipment.
